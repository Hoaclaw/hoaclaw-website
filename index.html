A cluster of agents

Specialists for every corner of HOA life.

Each agent is purpose-built for a domain. They share context, hand off cleanly, and ask before doing anything irreversible.

Resident Communications

Answers resident questions over email, SMS, or WhatsApp — grounded in your CC&Rs, bylaws, and meeting minutes.

Violation Workflow

Drafts notices, tracks cure deadlines, schedules hearings — with board approval at every escalation.

Dues & Collections

Sends reminders, calculates late fees per your governing docs, and negotiates payment plans before escalation.

Work Orders & Vendors

Routes maintenance requests to the right vendor, tracks SLAs, and keeps COIs and licenses current.

Governance & Meetings

Drafts agendas, sends legally-compliant notices, transcribes meetings, and manages elections end-to-end.

Board Reporting

Monthly packets, variance reports, delinquency trends — written in plain English for non-technical board members.

You stay in control.

Low-stakes tasks — reminders, routing, document retrieval — happen on their own. Anything with real consequences (violation notices, liens, special assessments) lands in your approval queue first. Graduated autonomy, by design.
